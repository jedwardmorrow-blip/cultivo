## Key Decisions

1. **Positioning Focus: Operational Intelligence, Not Compliance**
   - Rationale: Compliance is table stakes; legacy systems are already compliant with Metrc. CultOps differentiator is real-time visibility, cost-per-unit analytics, and ease of use.
   - Evidence: BioTrackTHC customers don't leave for "better compliance"—they leave for speed and insights.

2. **Arroya as Complementary Partner, Not Competitor**
   - Rationale: CultOps handles operations + production workflows; Arroya handles distribution + relationships. Natural partnership with no feature overlap.
   - Positioning: "Seed to Shelf: Visibility to Profitability" (CultOps handles operations; Arroya handles distribution)

3. **Revenue Model for Arroya: Three Streams**
   - Implementation services (40–50% margin): Data migration, workflow optimization, training
   - Support & success services (60–70% margin): Dedicated account management, custom integrations, reporting
   - Bundled offering (variable margin): "Arroya + CultOps" package with preferential pricing

4. **Competitive Positioning Matrix: Operations vs. Compliance**
   - Rationale: Two-axis positioning shows CultOps as the only platform balancing both operational capability AND regulatory compliance. BioTrackTHC is compliance-focused but operationally weak; LeafLogix is operationally strong (for retail) but not designed for production; Metrc tools are fragmented.

## Key Messaging Takeaways (For Sales Use)

### Lead with Operational Intelligence, Not Compliance
*"You probably already have Metrc. What you don't have is visibility into unit economics and real-time bottlenecks."*

### Use Production Workflows as Proof Points
*"Show me how BioTrackTHC's trim session UI saves you time. We built ours around your actual workflow."*

### Emphasize Cost Per Unit, Not Inventory Tracking
*"How much does it cost you to produce one unit of sellable flower? CultOps answers this automatically; legacy systems require manual calculation."*

### Position as Modern Alternative, Not Compliance Upgrade
*"BioTrackTHC customers don't leave for better compliance (they're already compliant). They leave for ease of use, speed, and insights they can't get elsewhere."*
