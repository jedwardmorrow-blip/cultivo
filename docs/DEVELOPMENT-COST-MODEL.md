## Executive Summary

CultOps requires **$2.5M capital** to achieve profitability by Month 18 (Q3 2027). This model details month-by-month operating burn across all functions, scaling scenarios, infrastructure costs, and sensitivity analysis for upside/downside revenue cases.

**Key Findings:**
- **Conservative burn path:** $600K (Months 1–6) → $440K (Months 6–12) → $85K (Months 12–18)
- **Unit economics remain strong** across all scenarios (LTV/CAC 71:1, 3.3-month payback)
- **AI/Agent budget controls** critical risk mitigated with framework; $80K Year 1 cap with quarterly gates
- **Path to profitability clear:** Month 18 shows monthly revenue ($160K+) exceeding monthly burn ($140K)
- **Sensitivity analysis:** Downside case (50% revenue miss) extends runway to 22 months; upside case (150% revenue) reaches profitability Month 12

## SECTION 7: AI/AGENT BUDGET CONTROLS (CRITICAL)

### AI Agent Spending Framework

**Total Year 1 Budget:** $80K (capped)
**Monthly Allocation:** $6.7K average (variable by use case)

| Use Case | Estimated Y1 Cost | Monthly Cap | ROI Threshold | Owner | Quarterly Gate |
|----------|------------------|-------------|---------------|-------|-----------------|
| **Dev Acceleration** (code generation, testing) | $25K | $2–3K | 4:1 | CTO | Yes |
| **Customer-Facing** (support, METRC integration help) | $20K | $1.5–2K | 3:1 | VP Ops | Yes |
| **Data Analysis** (cost modeling, projections, reporting) | $18K | $1.5K | 3:1 | CFO | Yes |
| **Ops/Admin** (process automation, document management) | $12K | $0.8–1K | 3:1 | Operations | Yes |
| **Experiments/Prototyping** | $5K | $0.4K | 2:1 (lower bar) | CTO | Ad-hoc |

**Total:** $80K

### Governance & Escalation

- **Quarterly Reviews:** Each owner submits YTD spend + ROI analysis; approvers sign off (CEO, CFO, CTO)
- **Monthly Tracking:** Run-rate spend vs. budget in CFO dashboard (visible to board)
- **Escalation Triggers:**
  - Spend exceeds 110% of monthly allocation → escalate to CFO for approval
  - Spend exceeds 125% of quarterly allocation → escalate to CEO for action
  - Any use case fails to achieve 3:1 ROI → reduce allocation 50% next quarter
- **Clawback Policy:** If Year 1 spend exceeds $80K, clawed back from contingency budget (forces trade-offs with other priorities)

**Owner:** CFO (responsible for tracking, escalation, and quarterly reviews)
