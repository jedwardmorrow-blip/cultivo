## The 3-Tier Pricing Model

### 📍 Tier 1: Single Facility (Starter)
**$4,000/month | $40K/year (with 3% annual discount)**

**Target:** Small cultivators (1 facility, <10,000 lbs/month)

**What's Included:**
- Cultivation planning & session management
- Post-production workflow (trim, packaging)
- Batch-centric inventory tracking
- Up to 5 internal users
- Email support
- METRC integration (standard)

**Why Customers Buy:** Eliminates spreadsheets + manual METRC submission. Typical ROI: 60–90 days (labor savings alone).

---

### 📍 Tier 2: Multi-Facility (Growth)
**$6,500/month base + $1,500/additional facility | $78K–$102K/year**

**Target:** Mid-size operators (2–4 facilities, $100K–$500K/month wholesale revenue)

**What's Included:**
- Everything in Tier 1
- Multi-facility consolidation & analytics (single P&L view)
- Order management & fulfillment workflows
- Delivery routing & optimization
- Up to 15 internal users
- Phone + email support (2-hour SLA)
- Custom METRC integration
- Cost-per-unit analytics dashboard

**Example:** 3 facilities = $6.5K + $3K (2 additional) = $9.5K/month = $114K/year

**Why Customers Buy:** Visibility across operations. Identify margin leaks, optimize distribution, reduce manual coordination.

---

### 📍 Tier 3: Enterprise (Scale)
**Custom pricing: typically $10K–$20K+/month | Minimum $12K/month**

**Target:** Large operators (4+ facilities, $1M+/month wholesale) OR distributors/partners

**What's Included:**
- Everything in Tier 2
- Unlimited facilities & users
- Dedicated account manager & technical lead
- Custom API integrations & data exports
- Priority feature development input
- Phone + Slack support (30-minute SLA)
- White-label reporting & branding
- Quarterly business reviews

**Example Arroya Partnership:** 12-month commitment: $15K/month = $180K/year

**Why Customers Buy:** Becomes the operating system for their business. API integrations with existing systems. Custom reporting for investors/lenders.

## Discounting & Negotiation Guidelines

### DO Offer:
- ✅ **Annual prepay discount:** 3–5% (improves cash flow)
- ✅ **Volume discount for multi-facility:** Already built in ($1.5K/facility vs. $4K if separate)
- ✅ **Introductory pricing (Month 1–3):** Free trial or 50% discount for Arroya pilot customers
- ✅ **Expansion credits:** If Tier 1 → Tier 2 upsell, apply 1–2 months credit to new tier

### DON'T Offer:
- ❌ **Deep discounts for "strategic" customers:** Unit economics depend on 60%+ gross margin
- ❌ **Revenue share instead of license fees:** Unpredictable; makes forecasting impossible
- ❌ **Unlimited custom development:** Creates unlimited service costs; use professional services pricing instead

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can we discount for 2-year commitments?**
A: Yes. Offer 5–10% discount for 2-year agreements (improves LTV, reduces churn risk).

**Q: What if the customer wants à la carte modules?**
A: Not recommended. Tiers are designed to be modular already. If they want 1–2 features from Tier 3, upsell Tier 2 + premium support instead.

**Q: Can we offer a free trial?**
A: Yes, for Arroya pilot customers (60–90 days free, then full pricing). NOT for direct sales (extend sales cycle, creates discount anchoring).

**Q: What about educational pricing / non-profits?**
A: Offer 25% discount for non-profit cannabis research institutions. Limit to 1–2 customers/year.
