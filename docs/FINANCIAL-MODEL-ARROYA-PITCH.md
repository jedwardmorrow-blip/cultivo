## SECTION 1: SaaS PRICING STRATEGY

### Market Context
- **TAM:** Cannabis operators (cultivation + distribution) spending $2K–$15K/month on fragmented tools
- **Competitors:** BioTrackTHC ($500–$2K/mo for compliance); LeafLogix ($1K–$3K/mo for retail); custom integrations ($10K+ build)
- **CultOps Positioning:** Premium consolidation play. One platform replaces 4–5 legacy systems. ROI: 60–90 days (manual entry savings alone).

### Pricing Tiers (SaaS Only)

#### Tier 1: Single Facility (Starter)
- **Price:** $4,000/month
- **Target:** Small cultivators (1 facility, <10,000 lbs/month flower production)
- **Includes:**
  - Cultivation planning & session management
  - Post-production workflow (trim, packaging)
  - Batch-centric inventory
  - Up to 5 internal users
  - Email support
  - METRC integration (standard)
- **Annual Contract:** $40K (3% discount if signed annually)

#### Tier 2: Multi-Facility (Growth)
- **Price:** $6,500/month base + $1,500/additional facility
- **Target:** Mid-size operators (2–4 facilities, $100K–$500K/month wholesale revenue)
- **Includes:**
  - Everything in Tier 1
  - Multi-facility consolidation & analytics
  - Order management & fulfillment workflows
  - Delivery routing & optimization
  - Up to 15 internal users
  - Phone + email support (2-hour response SLA)
  - Custom METRC integration (Arroya-specific setup)
  - Cost-per-unit analytics dashboard
- **Annual Contract:** $78K–$102K (depending on facility count; 5% discount if annual)
- **Example:** 3 facilities = $6.5K + $3K (2 additional) = $9.5K/month = $114K/year

#### Tier 3: Enterprise (Scale)
- **Price:** Custom (typically $10K–$20K+/month)
- **Target:** Large operators (4+ facilities, $1M+/month wholesale revenue) OR distributors (Arroya partnership)
- **Includes:**
  - Everything in Tier 2
  - Unlimited facilities & users
  - Dedicated account manager & technical lead
  - Custom API integrations & data exports
  - Priority feature development input
  - Phone + Slack support (30-minute response SLA)
  - White-label reporting & branding options
  - Quarterly business reviews
- **Minimum Commitment:** $12K/month or custom annual contract
- **Example Arroya Partnership:** 12-month minimum commitment of $15K/month = $180K/year

### Pricing Rationale
- **Tier 1** covers cost of infrastructure (~$800/mo per customer) + support (~$400/mo avg) + 60% gross margin (software standard)
- **Tier 2** leverages multi-facility consolidation as value driver; additional facility cost is minimal (incremental support + METRC integrations)
- **Tier 3** captures enterprise customers + distribution partners who derive outsized value from reporting, integrations, and account management
- **Competitor Benchmarking:** We're 2–3x cost of BioTrackTHC (~$1.5K/mo), but eliminate need for 4 other systems (~$5K–$8K/mo combined)

## SECTION 6: AI AGENT BUDGET FRAMEWORK (Critical Risk Mitigation)

### Current State
- **Problem:** No agent budget controls; CTO flagged as critical risk
- **Risk:** Unchecked AI/agent costs could exceed engineering budget by 10–20%
- **Impact:** Blows runway; derails Year 1 projections

### Proposed Framework

#### Budget Allocation
**Total CultOps Operating Budget (Year 1): $2.5M**
- Engineering: $680K (27%)
- Sales/Marketing: $400K (16%)
- Operations: $200K (8%)
- **AI/Agent Budget: $80K (3.2%) ← CAPPED**
- Runway/Contingency: $1.14M (46%)

#### AI Agent Spending Controls

**Rule 1: Tier by Use Case**
| Use Case | Monthly Budget | Examples |
|----------|----------------|----------|
| **Dev Acceleration** | $3K–$5K | Code gen, PR reviews, documentation automation |
| **Customer-Facing** | $2K–$4K | Support chatbot, report generation, predictive insights |
| **Ops/Admin** | $1K–$2K | Task automation, scheduling, data reconciliation |
| **Experiments** | $500–$1K | A/B testing new features, market research |
| **TOTAL MONTHLY CAP** | **$10K** | **Hard stop at $10K/month** |

**Rule 2: Quarterly Review & Re-allocation**
- End of Q1/Q2/Q3: Review actual vs. budget
- If on track: roll forward cap
- If exceeded: CTO + CFO review; must justify overages or cut features
- Annual adjustment: Based on Year 1 revenue performance

**Rule 3: ROI Threshold**
- Every agent-powered feature must show **>3:1 ROI** within 90 days
  - Example: Chatbot saves 5 hours/week of support labor ($5K/month value) → agent cost must be <$1.5K/month
- Features that don't hit ROI threshold are sunset

**Rule 4: Escalation Path**
- CTO alerts CFO if projected spend >110% of monthly budget
- CFO can approve up to 125% with board notification
- Above 125% = halt new features until next quarter

## SECTION 10: RISKS & MITIGATION

### Key Risks & Mitigations

#### Risk 1: Sales Cycle Longer Than Projected
**Impact:** Year 1 revenue = $150K instead of $386K; extends runway burn
**Mitigation:**
- Lock Arroya partnership in Month 3 (reduces sales risk to partner-driven pipeline)
- Offer 3-month free trial to Arroya customers (accelerates decision)
- Target early-adopter segment (growth-minded operators, <10 employees; faster decision cycles)

#### Risk 2: Product Integration Failures (with Arroya Systems)
**Impact:** Arroya partnership stalls; customers churn
**Mitigation:**
- Assign dedicated DevOps engineer to Arroya integration (Month 3–9)
- Establish joint integration roadmap with Arroya tech team (aligned quarterly)
- Phase integrations: Phase 1 (pilot: manual exports), Phase 2 (API webhooks), Phase 3 (real-time sync)

#### Risk 3: Uncontrolled AI/Agent Costs Exceed Budget
**Impact:** Reduces runway by 2–3 months; forces cost cuts mid-year
**Mitigation:** ✅ **AI Agent Budget Framework (Section 6)** + quarterly review gates

#### Risk 4: Engineering Attrition (losing key talent mid-year)
**Impact:** Feature delays; sales velocity slows; Arroya pilot at risk
**Mitigation:**
- Offer 0.1–0.5% equity to each engineer (vesting: 4 years, 1-year cliff)
- Establish clear career path: IC → Tech Lead → Manager
- Monthly 1–1s focused on growth & learning opportunities

#### Risk 5: Competitive Response from BioTrackTHC or New Entrant
**Impact:** Market pressures; slower customer acquisition; pricing pressure
**Mitigation:**
- Lock Arroya partnership ASAP (exclusive in certain segments)
- Build 12-month moat: METRC integration depth, mobile app, predictive analytics
- Monitor competitor moves quarterly; adjust positioning as needed
