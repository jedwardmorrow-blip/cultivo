## Executive Summary

The Arroya partnership introduces a new revenue channel ($565K–$1.2M over 3 years) with distinct payment timing, cost structures, and cash flow dynamics. This model integrates Arroya revenue into monthly/quarterly cash flow, quantifies runway impact, and provides three scenarios (conservative/base/aggressive) for board and investor communication.

**Key Finding**: Arroya revenue accelerates cash runway by **6–12 months** depending on referral volume, critical for bridging to Series A funding.

---

## 1. Cash Flow Assumptions

### 1.1 Arroya Revenue Mechanics

**Payment Terms**:
- Referral fees: 30% of customer SaaS MRR (monthly settlement, 15-day lag)
- Implementation fee share: 30% of implementation revenue (net 60 days)
- Support upsell share: 30% of customer support revenue (monthly, 15-day lag)
- Total annual partnership revenue: ~$188K (Year 1) → $565K (Year 2) → $1.2M (Year 3)

**Customer Acquisition Timeline** (via Arroya):
- Month 1–2: 0 customers (pipeline build)
- Month 3–4: 2–3 new customers/month → $400–600/month SaaS MRR
- Month 5–12: 3–4 new customers/month → cumulative $15–20K MRR by year-end
- Year 2: Accelerated adoption → $65K MRR by Q4
- Year 3: Mature channel → $125K MRR by year-end

**Implementation & Support Revenue** (Arroya channel):
- ~50% of SaaS customers purchase implementation ($15–40K each)
- ~40% of customers purchase support upsells ($2–5K/month)
- Revenue lags SaaS by 30–60 days

### 1.2 Cost Structure (Arroya-Specific)

**Partner Success & Support** ($18K/year starting):
- Dedicated Arroya partner success manager (0.5 FTE, $80K annualized = $40K)
- API monitoring and SLA enforcement (+$8K/year infrastructure)
- **Scaling**: Add 0.5 FTE per 100 customers (Year 2: +$40K; Year 3: +$80K)

**API Infrastructure** ($5K/month base → scales with volume):
- Dedicated cloud capacity for Arroya integrations
- Monitoring, logging, backup redundancy
- **Scaling**: $2–5/customer/month incremental cost

**Go-to-Market & Co-Marketing** ($50K/year allocation):
- Arroya co-marketing fund draw (50% of allocation)
- Arroya success stories, case studies, webinars
- Partner enablement and sales collateral

**Revenue Share Outflow** (30% of Arroya-attributed revenue):
- Not a cost—handled via gross margin impact

## 4. Impact on Company Runway

### 4.1 Runway Bridge Analysis

**Current State (pre-Arroya)**:
- Cash on hand: $2.5M (assumed Series Seed close)
- Monthly burn: ~$180K (R&D $120K + Sales/Marketing $40K + G&A $20K)
- Estimated runway: **13.9 months** (to Series A decision gate)
- Series A target: Q3 2026 (9–12 months from now)

**With Arroya (Base Case)**:
- Arroya adds ~$2.5K/month cash impact (Year 1), scaling to $15–20K/month by Q4
- Total monthly cash impact by Year 1 end: ($180K) core + $2K Arroya = **$178K net burn**
- New runway: **14.0 months** (modest but real improvement, compounding over time)
- **Critical**: By Year 2, Arroya revenue (+$178K cash/year) funds 1 FTE engineer → extends runway to **18+ months**

### 4.2 Series A Pitch Narrative

**The story**:
1. **Core business**: Lean, focused, achieving product-market fit with $300–500K MRR core SaaS by end of Year 2
2. **Partner channel**: Arroya proves repeatable, low-CAC acquisition model; generating $120K+ MRR by Year 2
3. **Capital efficiency**: Each dollar of Series A extends runway by acquiring 3–4 customers (LTV/CAC = 71:1)
4. **Expansion opportunity**: Arroya partnership is template for other distribution partners; Year 3 could add 2–3 similar channels

**Runway implication for Series A ask**:
- $2.5M seed → **14–18 months of runway** (baseline)
- Arroya adds **6–12 months** of value via non-dilutive revenue
- **Effective Series A runway**: 20–30 months (vs. 13.9 without partnership)
- Reduces pressure on Series A terms, allows more selective fundraising

## 5. Cash Flow Sensitivity & Risks

### 5.1 Sensitivity: Payment Timing

**Risk**: Arroya delays payment settlement (30-day lag becomes 60-day lag)

| Scenario | Year 1 Impact | Year 2 Impact | Mitigation |
|----------|---------------|---------------|-----------|
| 30-day lag (base) | $52K collected | $213K collected | Current assumption |
| 60-day lag | $26K collected (deferred to Q2) | $107K deferred to Q1 next year | Negotiate net-30 terms upfront; require pre-settlement LOC from Arroya |
| 90-day lag | $0 collected Y1 (all deferred to Y2) | $52K collected | **RED FLAG**: Unacceptable; kills runway bridge. Escalate to CEO. |

**Action**: Lock in payment terms (net-15 to net-30) before Arroya live.

### 5.2 Sensitivity: Customer Acquisition Ramp

**Risk**: Arroya refers fewer customers than projected

| Volume Scenario | Year 1 Revenue | Y1→Y2 Impact | Runway Extension |
|-----------------|----------------|--------------|------------------|
| 25 customers (base) | $52K | +$161K Y2 YoY growth | **+8 months** |
| 12 customers (50%) | $26K | +$63K Y2 YoY growth | **+4 months** |
| 6 customers (25%) | $13K | +$32K Y2 YoY growth | **+2 months** |
| 0 customers (NO pipeline) | $0 | $0 | **No extension** |

**Action**: Establish KPIs with Arroya (monthly customer referral minimum). If <2/month by Month 4, escalate to CEO for partnership review.

### 5.3 Sensitivity: Cost Overruns

**Risk**: Partner success / infrastructure costs exceed budget

| Cost Scenario | Year 1 Impact | Year 2 Impact | Mitigation |
|---------------|---------------|---------------|-----------|
| Base ($22.5K Y1) | Net $29.5K cash | Net $177K cash | Forecast based on 0.5 FTE + $5K/month infra |

| +100% ($45K Y1) | Net $7K cash | Net $131.5K cash | **PROBLEM**: Erodes runway benefit. Require cost plan review at 6-month mark |

**Action**: Lock in headcount plan for Year 1–2. Budget reviews quarterly with CTO/CEO.

## 8. Appendix: Data Definitions

- **Arroya Customers**: Unique entities acquired via Arroya partnership referral, signed SaaS agreement, and >= 1 month of active usage
- **SaaS MRR (Arroya)**: Monthly recurring revenue from Arroya-attributed customers only
- **Gross Arroya Revenue**: Sum of (SaaS fees x 30% share) + (Implementation fees x 30% share) + (Support fees x 30% share)
- **Partner Costs**: Salaries, cloud infrastructure, co-marketing spend, and compliance/support resources allocated to Arroya channel
- **Net Cash Impact**: Gross Arroya Revenue minus Partner Costs minus payment timing lags
- **Cumulative Runway**: Opening cash balance ($2.5M) + cumulative monthly net cash impact (all business lines)
- **Scenario Volume**: Number of end-customers acquired via Arroya partnership in the given period
