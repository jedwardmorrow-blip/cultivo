## Executive Summary

Board must choose one of three revenue share tiers for Arroya partnership. All options protect unit economics (3.3-month customer payback). **Recommendation: Option A (tiered 15%→12%→10%)** balances growth incentives with margin protection.

---

## Three Revenue Share Options

### OPTION A: Tiered Revenue Share ⭐ **RECOMMENDED**

| Year 1 Cumulative Revenue | Revenue Share % |
|---|---|
| $0–$250K | 15% |
| $250K–$500K | 12% |
| >$500K | 10% |

**Rationale:** Incentivizes high-volume customer acquisition while protecting margins as volume scales. Monthly calculation applies tier based on year-to-date cumulative revenue.

**Best for:** Growth-focused approach; aligns with Year 1 base-case projection of $300K (conservative) to $400K (aggressive).

---

### OPTION B: Fixed 12% Revenue Share

**Structure:** Flat 12% referral fee on all partnership revenue

**Rationale:** Simpler accounting; predictable Arroya cost structure for planning

**Best for:** If Arroya negotiates for simplicity or we forecast lower volume (<$250K Year 1)

---

### OPTION C: Fixed 10% + Performance Bonus

**Structure:**
- Base 10% referral fee on all partnership revenue
- Additional 2% bonus if annual revenue exceeds $500K (paid Q1 following year)

**Rationale:** Aggressive growth signal; aligns incentives toward $500K+ ramp

**Best for:** If we want to demonstrate confidence in partnership upside to Arroya

---

## Financial Impact Summary (Year 1 Base Case)

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Monthly partnership revenue (avg) | ~$25K |
| Monthly Arroya referral (at 12% tier) | ~$3K |
| Cumulative Year 1 revenue | $300K–$400K |
| Year 1 total Arroya payout | $36K–$48K |
| Cash flow impact (net positive) | $29.5K–$46K |
| Runway extension toward Series A | +6–10 months |

**Unit Economics:** All three tiers maintain 71:1 LTV/CAC ratio and 3.3-month customer payback. No option materially impacts profitability.

## Recommendation

**Choose Option A (Tiered 15%→12%→10%)** because:

1. **Growth incentive:** 15% tier motivates high-volume customer acquisition in early months
2. **Margin protection:** Tiers transition down as volume increases, protecting gross margin scaling
3. **Competitive:** Standard in SaaS partnerships; signals maturity to Arroya
4. **Flexibility:** Allows Arroya to propose modifications (thresholds, percentages) within negotiation window (April 9–12)
5. **Revenue-aligned:** Aligns with our financial projections ($300K–$400K Year 1); delivers $36K–$48K partnership payout while extending Series A runway 6–10 months

**Alternative if Arroya pushes back:** Fall back to Option B (fixed 12%) as compromise, or Option C if we want aggressive growth signal.
