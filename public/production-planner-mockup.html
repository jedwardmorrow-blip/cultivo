Honest assessment  ·  build state, 2026-04-29

What is shown reads from Cultivo-native session capture only. Tier A data is live: bucking yield split (n=173 sessions), packaging mix (n=248 sessions), order book and price realized (n=800 lines), 14 strains with ≥3 conversion runs.

Sprint 1A landed 2026-04-29: dropped seeded global defaults from the 128-strain table. The "1,082 wet g / plant" number is computed live from harvest_sessions, not seeded. Numbers without lower-case sigils carry n ≥ 5 and are demo-grade. Sigils n / m / l flag where the planner falls back to room-level signal until per-strain sample size lands.

Sprint 1B (6-8 weeks): per-stream conversion ratios (rosin yield, fresh-frozen wash yield), water activity at bucking, derived cost layer per strain × room × cycle. Sprint N+ optional add-ons remain explicitly out of scope for the core build: substrate telemetry, Metrc compliance, sensor integrations.
